Lawyers & Staff

Our attorneys have devoted their careers to one field of law: Social Security Disability. Get to know the people who will stand beside you.

Moe GreenbergPartner

Moe Greenberg

Partner & Co-Founder

Moe Greenberg co-founded the firm in 1991 with the belief that people with disabilities deserve an advocate who understands nothing but Social Security Disability law. More than three decades later, that mission still defines his practice.

Moe has personally guided thousands of clients through every stage of the disability process — from the initial application, through reconsideration and hearings, all the way to federal court when necessary. Clients know him for his steady guidance and his refusal to let a deadline slip.

James GannonPartner

James Gannon

Partner

James Gannon is a partner at Green & Greenberg who represents disability claimants from their first application through the appeals process and into federal court. He is known for the meticulous way he builds a medical record so that a claim is as strong as possible before it ever reaches a judge.

James takes the time to understand each client's day-to-day limitations and translate them into the language the Social Security Administration requires. His preparation is a big reason clients feel confident walking into their hearings.

Kevin HeitkeHearing Attorney

Kevin Heitke

Hearing Attorney

Kevin Heitke represents clients where it matters most — at the hearing before an administrative law judge. He prepares each client thoroughly so they know what to expect, and he presents the evidence in a way that makes the strongest possible case for benefits.

Approachable and thorough, Kevin makes sure clients never feel alone in the hearing room. His focus is on turning a well-built record into an approval.
