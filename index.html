The Northeast's dedicated Social Security Disability firm.

That single focus means every attorney and staff member on your case lives and breathes SSDI and SSI. We know the local judges, the medical experts, and exactly what the Social Security Administration needs to approve a claim.

Why Choose a Local Firm

Five reasons clients trust Green & Greenberg.

Unlike national firms that practice in many different jurisdictions, Green & Greenberg is a local firm that benefits from its unique position in the community. Hiring a local firm — rather than an out-of-state national firm — comes with real advantages. Tap any reason to read more.

We have physical offices in and around Providence, RI, allowing in-person consultations and giving us geographical proximity to the local district offices, Disability Determination Services offices, and hearing offices that handle your claim.

We focus exclusively on SSI and SSDI claims — not Workers' Comp or Personal Injury. This gives us deep expertise in a very specialized field, and it has been the entire focus of our practice since 1991.

The judges who hear your claim control the ultimate outcome of your case. Green & Greenberg is familiar with the judges who will preside over your case, and the firm makes informed decisions on how to most effectively proceed and submit evidence and arguments — based on years of experience in this particular jurisdiction. We understand that here in New England, one size does not fit all.

Because Green & Greenberg is not a national firm, we have a smaller local caseload that allows us to concentrate on quickly updating the medical record and presenting pre- and post-hearing written legal arguments for an expedited decision on your claim.

Green & Greenberg has established relationships with local doctors, clinics, and vocational experts, making it easier to gather the information needed to support your claim and build the strongest possible record.
